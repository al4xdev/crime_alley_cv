# Company Research: Pareto

## Profile & Size
- **Sector**: Artificial Intelligence, Multi-Agent Platforms, Marketing Automation (RPA & GenAI)
- **Rough Headcount**: Estimated between 50 and 200 employees
- **Funding/Maturity**: Founded in 2012/2013 in Brazil. Transitioned from digital marketing automation (RPA) to generative AI agents (developing Tess AI, which spun off as an independent company in November 2024). Invested and supported by Google (via Google for Startups Accelerator).

## Tech Stack (public signals)
- **Programming Languages**: Python, TypeScript/JavaScript
- **Backend / Databases**: FastAPI, Flask, Node.js, SQLAlchemy, PostgreSQL, Redis
- **Frontend**: React, Vite, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS
- **AI & Integrations**: Model Context Protocol (MCP), MCP Servers, AI Gateways, Multi-Agent systems, orchestrating large models (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.) and legacy systems (ERP, CRM)
- **Infrastructure**: Docker, cloud-native deployments with security-first environments

## Culture & Values
- **Time Back Concept**: Using automation and AI to eliminate repetitive manual work, returning time to professionals.
- **Enterprise Security**: High emphasis on security, data privacy, and compliance for corporate clients.
- **Innovation & Autonomy**: Culture of constructing autonomous digital workers/agents collaborating in unified workspaces.

## Open Roles (recent)
- **Engenheiro(a) / Arquiteto(a) de Plataforma IA & MCP**: Highlights their priority to build and scale corporate AI orchestration systems, develop custom MCP Servers, and implement advanced multi-agent workflows.

## Recent News
- **Tess AI Spin-off**: In late 2024, Tess AI officially spun off from Pareto Group to act as an independent AI agent/hub company.
- **G2 Recognition**: Tess AI ranked as a leader in G2 categories for AI Agent orchestration and multi-model hubs.
- **Google Support**: Ongoing collaboration and acceleration programs with Google for Startups.
